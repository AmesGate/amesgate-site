Who we are

AmesGate is built around one simple idea: small businesses deserve enterprise-level discipline without enterprise-level nonsense.

What AmesGate is

A practical IT partner for small teams that need stability, security, and someone to own the outcome. We focus on Microsoft 365, devices, networks, backups, and “the stuff that keeps the business running.”

Philosophy: clear plan → clean execution → documentation → maintenance.

What AmesGate isn’t

We’re not a “call center MSP” that rotates techs and closes tickets fast just to hit metrics. We’re also not here to sell you tools you don’t need.

Goal: fewer surprises, less chaos, more control.

What you can expect

Plain communication, written plans, realistic timelines, and systems that don’t fall apart.

Bonus: you’ll actually know what you own and how it works.

Where we work

Colorado-based. Remote support when possible. On-site when it matters.

Serving: small offices, clinics, shops, and teams that just need it handled.
